Using the arrow keys or by clicking on the screen, move around Durham to look for eggs. You can move the camera about by clicking and dragging

Eggs are not visible until you are close to them, and will only appear as you drag the screen to look around!

When you see an egg, click on it! A form will appear, which should already have your name and email address filled in. Click submit to capture this egg. It will then disappear from the game for all players, so no one else will be able to find it!

At the top of the screen you will see how many eggs remain in the game for players to find, and also how many eggs you have found yourself.

If streetview gets stuck, press refresh to go back to the starting point, don't worry though, you won't lose the eggs you have already found if you re-enter the same email address!
